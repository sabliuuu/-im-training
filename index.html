Flash sales, discount codes, product teasers, event promos, link traffic (link sticker)

Reels

VerticalAlgorithm-Driven15s–90s

Short-form video pushed heavily by IG's algorithm. Can reach way beyond the KOL's existing followers via Explore & Reels feed.短视频被 IG 算法大力推送，可通过探索页和 Reels 信息流触达远超 KOL 现有粉丝的受众。

Explore page distribution. Reaches non-followers. Most brands' #1 IG deliverable request.

Mid to high. Most common deliverable — use as the baseline when scoping IG campaigns.

Product education, tutorials, ingredient deep-dives, before/after, step-by-step guides

Highest saves per format. Algorithm re-shows carousels to non-engaged users. Great for skincare, finance, F&B.

Slightly lower than or equal to Reels. Often paired with long captions.

VerticalAlgorithm-Driven15s–10min

For You Page (FYP) — can go viral far beyond followers. Hook in first 2–3s is critical.

Core deliverable. Rate varies significantly by market and follower tier.

TikTok Live

Real-TimeE-Commerce Ready1–4 hrs

Real-time broadcast with in-app shopping. In TikTok Shop-active markets (TH, ID, VN), Live is a massive sales channel — KOLs sell while chatting live with viewers.实时直播结合应用内购物。在 TikTok Shop 成熟市场（泰国、印尼、越南），直播是重要销售渠道——KOL 边聊边卖货。

Product launches, flash sales, limited-time deals, brand takeovers

Product pinning in-stream. Real-time conversion. Viewer gifts/coins as engagement signal.

Flat fee + % of sales in TikTok Shop markets. Clarify the model before negotiations.

Always check average Live viewers first. Avoid KOLs with under 500 avg. Live viewers.

TikTok ShopTikTok Shop / 小黄车

E-CommerceAffiliate OR PaidTH · ID · VN · MY

The yellow cart icon lets viewers buy a product without leaving TikTok. KOLs pin products to their videos or Live streams. Most developed in Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, Malaysia.小黄车图标让观众无需离开 TikTok 即可直接下单。KOL 可将商品挂载到视频或直播中。目前在泰国、印尼、越南、马来西亚发展最成熟。

Affiliate Model (Commission)佣金分成模式

KOL earns % of each sale generated through their link. No upfront cost for brand. Risky: KOL may under-promote if product doesn't convert well.KOL 赚取通过其链接产生每笔销售额的佣金比例。品牌无需预付费用。风险：若产品转化率低，KOL 可能推广力度不足。

Typical commission: 5–20% depending on product margin典型佣金比例：5–20%，视产品利润率而定

Paid Flat Fee Model固定费用模式

KOL receives a fixed fee regardless of sales. More predictable for both parties. Most common model in agency-led campaigns.KOL 获得固定酬劳，与销售额无关。对双方来说更可预期。代理公司主导的项目中最常见的合作模式。

KOL has guaranteed income — usually more willing to put in effortKOL 收入有保障，通常更愿意认真推广

Note: Both models can coexist — KOL gets a flat fee PLUS sales commission. When writing the brief, always explicitly state which model applies. Never assume the KOL knows.⚠️ 两种模式可以并存 — KOL 同时获得固定酬劳 + 销售佣金。撰写 brief 时务必明确说明适用哪种模式，不要假设 KOL 自行了解。
YouTube is the platform for long-form, considered-purchase categories — tech, finance, gaming, health & wellness. Audience is more intent-driven and research-oriented. Strong in India, Philippines, and broadly across SEA.YouTube 是长视频、深度购买决策类别的首选平台——科技、金融、游戏、健康养生。受众更具购买意向和研究导向。在印度、菲律宾及整个东南亚表现强劲。

Dedicated Video

100% Brand Focus5–20 minHighest Cost

The entire video is solely about the brand or product. Maximum brand exposure. The creator's full production effort is dedicated to your campaign.整支视频百分之百聚焦于品牌或产品，品牌曝光最大化，创作者将全部制作精力投入你的项目。

Product launches, in-depth reviews, tutorials, brand storytelling campaigns

Full creative control negotiable. Videos rank on Google search — long shelf life (months to years).

Most expensive YT format. Premium for channel exclusivity.

Add content approval clause in contract. Confirm video won't be age-restricted or set to private post-publish.

Integration (Tap-in)

Native Feel60–120s SegmentBest ROI

Brand is woven into the creator's regular video as a sponsored segment. Feels like part of the content, not an ad. Also called "mid-roll sponsor" or "tap-in."品牌以赞助段落的形式融入创作者的常规视频，感觉像内容的一部分而非广告。也称 mid-roll 赞助或嵌入式植入。

App downloads, subscriptions, SaaS tools, brand awareness in tech/gaming/finance channels

More authentic than Dedicated. Creator's own content drives the traffic. Best placement: first 3 minutes for highest view-through.

30–50% lower than Dedicated. Best ROI on YouTube — the go-to choice for most campaigns.

You don't control surrounding content. Always screen the creator's recent videos for brand-safety before signing.

YouTube Shorts

Vertical<60sGrowing

YouTube's short-form vertical video. Growing in reach but creators earn significantly less from Shorts vs. long-form. The algorithm is still maturing.YouTube 短视频，覆盖面持续增长，但创作者收益远低于长视频。算法仍在完善阶段。

Top-of-funnel brand awareness, repurposing TikTok/Reels content, reaching younger demographics

Separate Shorts feed algorithm. Can reach non-subscribers. Good as a bonus alongside a Dedicated or Integration deal.

Lowest on YouTube. Often included free or at a nominal fee as a bundle add-on.

Don't buy Shorts-only deals. The audience intent is low. Use it as a top-funnel bonus only.

YouTube Live

Real-TimeNiche UseGaming · Events

Live broadcast. Less common for brand integrations vs TikTok Live. Mainly used in gaming, esports, or premium brand event livestreams where the audience is highly engaged.实时直播。品牌合作使用频率低于 TikTok Live，主要用于游戏、电竞或高黏性社群的品牌活动直播。

Gaming product launches, tech unboxings, brand-sponsored events with highly engaged communities

Stream is archived as a regular video — searchable long-term. Super Chat = extra engagement signal.

Varies by creator's live audience. Always verify average concurrent viewers before budgeting.

Most YT creators only go Live occasionally. Confirm availability and willingness upfront — don't assume.

YouTube Format Decision: Default to Integration for most campaigns (best ROI). Upgrade to Dedicated only for flagship launches needing full exposure. Add Shorts as a free/low-cost bonus. Reserve Live for gaming/tech/events specifically.📌 YouTube 格式决策：大多数项目默认选 Integration（性价比最高）。仅在旗舰发布需要最大曝光时升级为 Dedicated。Shorts 作为免费或低价附赠。Live 专用于游戏/科技/活动类型。

KOL Tiers (SEA general guide):KOL层级（东南亚通用指南）：
• Nano (1K–10K) — High engagement, niche audience, very affordable. Good for hyper-local or community campaigns.

• Macro (100K–1M) — Broader reach, slightly lower ER. Good for awareness campaigns at scale.
• Mega / Celebrity (1M+) — Maximum reach, premium pricing, ER can be lower. Use only for big brand launches.• Nano（1K–10K）— 互动率高，垂直小众受众，价格亲民，适合超本地化或社区型活动。

• Mega / 明星（1M+）— 最大覆盖，溢价定价，互动率可能偏低，仅用于大品牌发布。
